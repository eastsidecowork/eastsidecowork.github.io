Coworking on the east side of Rochester, NY

Welcome

We're building a community of people who live and work around the east side of Rochester, NY. Our mission is to provide a place for individuals and small companies to work and socialize in a shared office space.

Support us on Twitter @eastsidecowork.

Questions & Answers

What is coworking?

Coworking is many things. The most practical aspect is having an office to work in when you otherwise may not have one. From a financial point of view, it is much cheaper to be a member of a coworking space than to rent an office for yourself.

It's also about socializing. If you're someone who primarily works at home, a coworking space gives you the opportunity to have the social interaction you normally would have in a typical office.

In any case, it provides you a place to go if you need to get out of the house and need a quiet place to work with high speed Internet and free coffee.

Where are you located?

We currently do not have place to work. We are in the process of bootstrapping the business and are looking for people who are interested in coworking in Rochester.

Right now we meet up once a week for a coffee. If you're interested in coworking, please check out our calendar and join us for a coffee break.

The areas we are interested in setting up an office is Fairport and Penfield.

How much does it cost?

Since we don't have a location, we're going to hold off on setting prices.

In general, those who want to use the space on a daily basis would pay a monthly membership fee. We also plan on offering day passes that you can uses at your leisure. You will be able to purchase day passes one at time or in bulk.

We also plan on giving five free day passes to anyone who wants to get a feel for coworking before purchasing a membership or day passes.

Most importantly, members will be able to cancel their membership at any time with no penalty. Day passes won't be refundable.
