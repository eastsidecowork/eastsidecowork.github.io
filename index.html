WHAT IS COWORKING?

Professional Space

A real office. One you could bring a client to. No screaming kids in the background. No household chores competing for your attention. Be productive once again!

Budget Friendly

Monthly membership at East Side Cowork is much less expensive than hourly doses of over-priced coffee at a cafe.

Collaborative Space

Feeling isolated in your home office? Work around other creative and friendly professionals. Attend meetups. Or just chat around the "water cooler" with other adults.

Convenient location

East Side Cowork is just minutes from 490 in Perinton, across from Wegmans, and offers easy access from routes 31 or 250. There's a Post Office and many restaurants and shops nearby, plus it's just a short drive to route 96, Eastview Mall, and the NYS Thruway.

Our coworking space offers our members a collaborative, engaging work environment that provides an escape from the isolation of a work-at-home environment; one that is less distracting than a coffee shop; and one that is more productive than a hectic drive through rush hour traffic to a downtown location.

WHO WE ARE - WHAT WE'RE ABOUT

Our vision

Coworking is more than just an office. (Though ours is a great office with all the conveniences of a "real" office, plus coffee!) Coworking is really about the people and the connections we can make, the ways we inspire each other, and the knowledge we share.

Our experience

East Side Cowork is owned and operated by Tim Poulsen. I have worked from home for over 16 years. During that time, I've enjoyed the luxury of flexible hours, zero commute, and a casual dress code. But I've also been frustrated by isolation, distraction, and near-zero work/life balance.

I work full-time for Appcelerator, a Silicon Valley-based startup powering cross-platform mobile application development with their open source Titanium framework. I'm an active member of the tech/startup scene in Rochester. I owned & operated Skypanther Studios, a mobile and web development firm for many years. You will often find me at Startup Drinks, Rochester Open Coffee Club meetings, Bar Camp, and other local events.

Tim Poulsen - Owner

ALL PRICING DETAILS

  • Day Use: $10/day, 9-5 access Mon-Fri (except holidays, closures, etc.), includes coffee/tea/breakroom use
  • 10-pack of day passes: $90, includes all day-use level benefits
  • Monthly 9-5: $125/mo, includes all day-use level benefits, up to 4 hrs/mo conference room use
  • Monthly 24/7: $175/mo, 24/7 access with key deposit and signed key agreement, includes coffee/tea/breakroom use, up to 6 hrs/mo conference room use
  • Dedicated desk: $225/mo, 24/7 access with key deposit and signed key agreement, choose and reserve your desk, includes locking storage (file-cabinet or similar), plus coffee/tea/breakroom use, up to 8 hrs/mo conference room use
  • Virtual: $25/mo, use our mailing address, email notification of mail/packages received
  • Drop-in meeting: $10/hr (plus $2/person/hour)

Monthly memberships include use of our mailing address for letters & packages, email notification of mail received, listing on our web site (optional)
