The East Side (Cowork) Story

Do you work at home? A coffee shop? Library? Or are you just sick of the office doldrums. East Side Cowork (ESC) will be right for you. ESC will be a coworking center located in the Fairport/Perinton/Penfield area. ESC will offer our members a collaborative, engaging work environment that provides an escape from the isolation of a work-at-home environment; one that is less distracting than a coffee shop; and one that is more productive than a hectic drive through rush hour traffic to a downtown location.

Members will enjoy:

  • A stimulating environment with opportunities to socialize with other professionals
  • A professional atmosphere, with conference rooms, quiet areas, and space for meeting with clients with none of the distractions of home or noise of a coffee shop
  • Access to high-speed internet connections and standard office equipment like printers and photocopiers
  • A business mailing address (plus usually someone to sign for packages during business hours)
  • After work social time, “happy hours”, classes and events.
  • Members can host events at ESC.
  • Access to our library of books and reference materials
  • Free coffee! (and maybe some other refreshments)

What is Coworking?

Coworking is many things. The most practical aspect is having an office to work in when you otherwise may not have one. From a financial point of view, it is much cheaper to be a member of a coworking space than to rent an office for yourself.

It's also about socializing. If you're someone who primarily works at home, a coworking space gives you the opportunity to have the social interaction you normally would have in a typical office.

In any case, it provides you a place to go if you need to get out of the house and need a quiet place to work with high speed Internet and free coffee.
